# Labyrinth — Allure of Stone

[![Latest release](https://img.shields.io/github/v/release/JacquelynneHahn/Labyrinth-AllureOfStone?label=latest%20release&color=c9a227)](https://github.com/JacquelynneHahn/Labyrinth-AllureOfStone/releases/latest)

A native Android reader for the *Labyrinth — Allure of Stone* novel, with
chapter narration, live party / encounter / bestiary panels, a gallery of
story-moment art that unlocks as you read — and now **Android Auto**, so
the story rides with you.

This repository hosts release binaries — the source code lives elsewhere.

## Download

Grab `app-debug.apk` from the
[**latest release**](https://github.com/JacquelynneHahn/Labyrinth-AllureOfStone/releases/latest)
— it's the complete edition (~1.3 GB) with all prose, art, and narration
included. No additional downloads after install.

## Install on Android

1. Open the APK link above on your Android device, or transfer the file
   from a computer and tap it in your Files / Downloads app.
2. Android will ask whether to allow installs from this source. Approve
   for whichever app you used to open the APK (your browser or file
   manager).
3. Tap **Install**, then **Open**. Pick a save slot and start reading.

## Self-updates

The app keeps itself current. Shortly after launch on Wi-Fi it checks
this repository in the background; if a newer version is out, a gold
**"Update available"** banner slides down — tap it, confirm once with
Android's standard prompt, and you're on the new version.

To check manually any time: open the chapter selector at the top of the
reader → scroll to the bottom of the dropdown → tap
**v… • Check for updates**.

## Android Auto

The app appears among Android Auto's media apps — browse Part 1, Part 2,
and the Sacred Library from the car screen and play any narrated chapter
with cover art and full transport controls.

One-time setup on the phone (required for sideloaded apps):
**Settings → Android Auto → tap *Version* 10 times → Developer settings →
enable *Unknown sources*.**

## What's inside the app

- **The complete novel to date** — Parts 1 & 2 (Ch 1–42) with full
  word-by-word audio narration, plus **Part 3 — Kareth's Records**
  (Ch 43–67): the Sacred Library of journals, petitions, and the Books
  of the Temple.
- **Party tracking** — HP, MP, Stamina, Emotional Stamina, buffs and
  statuses, per beat.
- **Live combat ledger** with formulas, kill counts, and HP bars that
  drain as the fight unfolds.
- **Bestiary in the Enemies panel** — tap any enemy tile for the body
  shot and full lore.
- **Character bonds** — relationship tracking between Rob, Mirelle,
  Elara, and Johr.
- **Story-moment gallery** that fills in as you read.
- **Book activation per save slot** — Books 1 / 2 / 3 toggles in the
  profile picker.
- **Profiles** so multiple readers can each track their own progress.

## About the book

*Labyrinth — Allure of Stone* opens with Rob, a stranded medic, recruited
into the dangerous world of the labyrinth — a vast subterranean structure
that grants powers to those who survive its depths. The story follows him,
his guide Mirelle, and the companions they find as they navigate combat,
politics, and growing magical bonds across the city of Lethar and the
labyrinth's depths.
